Walking Battery

Self-sufficient power,
your partner in time of need

Introduction

Have you ever felt anxious when your smartphone battery runs out? The Walking Battery project provides a solution by generating electricity while walking.

Current Situation & Challenges

Mountaineers

Lack of electricity during climbing leads to communication issues and unusable devices.

Evacuees

Limited power in shelters and health risks due to inactivity.

Developing Countries

Many regions still lack stable electricity despite widespread smartphone use.

Existing Solutions Problems

  • Solar panels do not work at night or in bad weather
  • Hand generators are noisy and inefficient
  • Portable options are often heavy or inconvenient

Our Solution

Walking Battery generates electricity through walking using special shoes connected to a battery.

  • Generate power while walking
  • Works anytime, anywhere
  • Environmentally friendly
  • No need to pre-charge

Usage

The generated power can be used for mobile chargers, flashlights, and other essential devices.

Conclusion

Walking Battery aims to provide security and convenience by ensuring power is always available through simple daily movement.
